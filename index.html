Hello there!

I’m Honey Grace but you can call me your next favorite storyteller. Growing up with my grandparents, I picked up a love for old-fashioned values—like handwritten notes and heartfelt conversations—but I’ve since learned to embrace the modern world with open arms and an open mind. I’m the kind of person who thrives on deep connections, though I admit, I’m not the best at making the first move. My friends often tell me their first impression of me was a mix of “innocent,” “shy,” and “a little too bida-bida”—but that’s just the surface. Deep down, I’m someone who’s always ready to listen, share a laugh, and support others (just don’t ask me to manage your finances, haha!). When I’m not making new memories with my friends, you’ll find me in my favorite spot—a quiet room with music in the background and a stack of manga or manhwa by my side. In a nutshell, I’m a blend of tradition and modernity, a dreamer with her feet firmly on the ground, and always a work in progress. Let’s make this chapter of life unforgettable together!
